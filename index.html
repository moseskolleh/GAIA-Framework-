üìä Model Details & Specs ‚ñº
The GAIA Framework includes data for 18 different AI models from major providers including OpenAI, Anthropic, Meta, and DeepSeek. Each model has detailed specifications including energy consumption (Wh), water usage (L), and carbon emissions (gCO2e) per query.
üî¨ Assessment Methodology ‚ñº
Our assessment methodology calculates environmental impact by multiplying base model metrics with task complexity and query volume factors. Environmental Score = (Energy/10 + Water*50 + Carbon*2). The framework uses PUE (Power Usage Effectiveness), WUE (Water Usage Effectiveness), and CIF (Carbon Intensity Factor) multipliers specific to each cloud provider.
üìñ Definitions & Acronyms ‚ñº
Wh (Watt-hour): Energy unit, 1 Wh ‚âà LED bulb for 6 minutes

PUE: Power Usage Effectiveness - Ratio of total facility energy to IT equipment energy.

CO2e: Carbon Dioxide Equivalent - A standard unit for measuring carbon footprint.

WUE: Water Usage Effectiveness - Measures cooling water efficiency in data centers.

CIF: Carbon Intensity Factor - Grid emissions per unit of energy.
